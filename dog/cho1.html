Origin of the breed for Shiba Inu

  • Shiba Inu dog originated from the land of the rising sun, is one of the six original dog breeds of Japan. The Shiba Inu is very agile, a breed suitable for living in the mountains. They have appeared since ancient times, becoming the symbol of Japan.

  • The Shiba Inu is similar in appearance to the Akita Inu, but slightly smaller in size. In addition, Shiba Inu is also featured with a bright smile on the corner of his mouth, very cute and friendly.

  • The Shiba Inu has been around since the 3rd century BC. They were originally bred to help humans hunt small animals, such as birds and rabbits. During World War II, the Shiba Inu was once in danger of extinction. They suffered from food shortages, and constant epidemics made Shiba Inu a reluctant source of food. So to this day, the Shiba Inu dog in Japan is the result of crossbreeding from 3 remaining Shiba dog breeds: Shinshu Shiba (dog breed from Nagano), Mino Shiba (from Gifu) and San'in Shiba (from Tottori and Shimane).

Appearance characteristics of the Shiba Inu

  • The body of the Shiba Inu varies in size between males and females. Male Shiba Inu is about 2kg bigger than female Shiba Inu, male dogs usually weigh 10kg while female dogs weigh about 8kg. The male Shiba Inu is about 35 to 43cm tall, and the female is about 33 to 41cm tall.

  • The Shiba Inu has a coat with two coats: the outer layer is thick, stiff and relatively smooth, and the inner layer is soft and shorter. The inner coat is the cilia, quite similar to the cilia of a fox.

  • The outer ruffled coat is usually 4 to 5cm long, in the double coat they will be longer, ruffled and raised like a cotton ball. Shiba Inu coat colors are quite rich and diverse, today the most popular are red, black, brown or sesame colors. The inner coat is usually cream, buff or gray, sometimes white.

  • The head of the Shiba Inu is quite large compared to the body. The head is always held high, in keeping with their straight back and solid build.

  • Shiba Inu face with small, black eyes, often with white tufts of hair just above the eyelids. The snout is pointed, quite wide, like it is always smiling. The ears are triangular, erect on the top of the head. Overview of the face gives them the sympathy of the viewer, looks very agile, cute and friendly.

Character characteristics of the Shiba Inu

  • Shiba Inu is a dog with a very independent personality, sometimes if it loses control, it will be quite aggressive, causing wars with other animals. Therefore, Shiba Inu should not be kept with many other pets in the family, especially small pets. It is also not advisable to let Shiba Inu play with young children unsupervised, because they can easily display aggression that causes undesirable situations to arise.

  • Although a bit rude and aggressive, in return Shiba Inu is an extremely loyal pet. They win absolute respect and protection for their owners, no matter what the situation they are ready to fight to protect their owners.

  • Shiba Inu dogs are very intelligent. They learn very quickly from the habits of their owners, just maintain certain actions, they will understand it is the right thing to do. So the process of training Shiba Inu dogs is quite simple. For example, if you want to teach them to go to the toilet, keep bringing them to the toilet after each meal, they will understand by themselves that this is where they are allowed to go to the toilet.

  • The Shiba Inu is a very meticulous and clean dog. They minimize the contamination of their beautiful fur, rarely see them lying on the ground. Shiba Inu dogs also have a habit of licking the soles of their paws like cats.
