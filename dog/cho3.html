Origin of Dalmatians

  • Dalmatians are also known as Dalmatian dogs. It is called after the Croatian region Dalmatia. This is the site where dalmatians were first discovered by humans.

  • The Dalmatian breed is ancestral to the giant Great Dane. Therefore, Dalmatians are also dogs with hound bloodline, aggressive nature. Over time, bred and domesticated by humans; They were gentler, more friendly.

  • When it first became popular in Europe, dalmatians were used as guard dogs for the nobility and merchants. They are still used in some places as hunting dogs, due to their keen sense of smell.

  • Today, dalmatians are used for many different purposes such as scenes, guards, circus acts, etc. In particular, dalmatians also make great contributions to fire prevention and fighting with firefighters.

Dalmatian appearance

  • Dalmatians are medium sized dogs. Adult Dalmatians are 56-61cm tall. Their weight will reach about 15-32 kg at maturity. The Dalmatian's body length is almost twice their height. Male Dalmatians always have many advantages in appearance and physical strength compared to female Dalmatians.

  • Considering the proportional relationship between the size and body measurements of Dalmatians, they are dogs with a slim, slim body. Their muscular system is extremely developed, especially the thighs, chest muscles, and shoulder muscles. Dalmatians' chests are wide and deep, giving them a muscular look. Dalmatians have a slim waist, a long, straight back, and a gentle slope towards the tail.

  • Dalmatians have small eyes, the eyes are located far apart. Their eye color is usually brown, but there are also dogs with blue eyes, amber eyes. Their ears are quite thin, hanging to the sides. The position of the ears is high and close to the head.

  • The coat of the Dalmatian is very short, tight to the skin. Dalmatians have many different coat colors but the most common and characteristic is the white coat with black spots. These adorable dogs with this coat color are often referred to as raisin puddings. Besides, the spots of Dalmatians can have other colors such as brown, gray, chocolate, ..

  • When young, there are spots with pure white feathers. As they grow up, they begin to appear dark spots on their bodies. The spots are usually 30-60mm in size. Dalmatians have 2 shedding seasons in a year.

Dalmatian's personality

Dalmatians have their own distinct personality traits, they are a favorite dog breed all over the world thanks to their personality traits.

  • Fun and active

  • Good memory

  • Loyal and smart
